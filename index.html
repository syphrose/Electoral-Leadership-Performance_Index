About Us

The Electoral & Leadership Performance Index (ELPI) is a leading organization dedicated to inspiring elected and appointed leaders across Africa to pursue excellence in leadership, governance and accountability. With a focus on assessing and measuring leadership performance; ELPI conducts credible research anchored and aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). ELPI envisions an Africa where leaders are motivated, recognized, and driven to excel in their roles. Through the development of reliable performance metrics, hosting thematic events, and providing ongoing Monitoring & Evaluation services, ELPI aims to foster leadership transformation across the continent, contributing to the achievement of the SDGs and promoting a culture of accountability and excellence.

Our Goals

Leadership excellence, transforming communities.

The Vision

To become the leading organization in Africa that catalyzes leadership excellence and accountability, creating a continent where leaders are motivated to exceed standards, embrace sustainable practices, and drive progress in line with global development goals.

The Mission

To inspire and promote excellence and accountability in African leadership by assessing, recognizing, and encouraging leaders in various sectors through credible performance metrics, research, and thematic events. ELPI is committed to fostering positive leadership transformations that align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

The Purpose

To provide a robust framework for evaluating and improving leadership across Africa by setting credible performance standards, offering monitoring and evaluation services, and celebrating achievements in key areas such as health, education, infrastructure, and governance. ELPI seeks to drive impactful change that benefits communities and advances sustainable development across the continent.
