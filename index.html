About Us

Electoral & Leadership Performance Index (ELPI) is a premier organization dedicated to inspiring leaders across electoral, government, non-governmental organizations, and corporate sectors in Africa to embrace and practice excellence and accountability. ELPI is uniquely positioned to assess and measure the performance of African leaders through credible research anchored around the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). ELPI envisions a future where leaders in Africa are inspired, recognized, and motivated to excel in their respective fields. By establishing credible performance measurement parameters, organizing thematic events, and offering ongoing Monitoring & Evaluation services, ELPI seeks to catalyze a positive transformation in leadership across the continent, ultimately contributing to the realization of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and fostering a culture of excellence and accountability.

Our Goals

Empowering Leaders, Transforming Communities.

The Vision

To become the leading organization in Africa that catalyzes leadership excellence and accountability, creating a continent where leaders are motivated to exceed standards, embrace sustainable practices, and drive progress in line with global development goals.

The Mission

To inspire and promote excellence and accountability in African leadership by assessing, recognizing, and encouraging leaders in various sectors through credible performance metrics, research, and thematic events. ELPI is committed to fostering positive leadership transformations that align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

The Purpose

To provide a robust framework for evaluating and improving leadership across Africa by setting credible performance standards, offering monitoring and evaluation services, and celebrating achievements in key areas such as health, education, infrastructure, and governance. ELPI seeks to drive impactful change that benefits communities and advances sustainable development across the continent.
