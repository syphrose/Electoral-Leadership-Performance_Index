About Us

Electoral & Leadership Performance Index (elpi) is a premier organization dedicated to inspiring leaders across electoral, government, non-governmental organizations, and corporate sectors in Africa to embrace and practice excellence and accountability. elpi is uniquely positioned to assess and measure the performance of African leaders through credible research anchored around the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). elpi envisions a future where leaders in Africa are inspired, recognized, and motivated to excel in their respective fields. By establishing credible performance measurement parameters, organizing thematic events, and offering ongoing Monitoring & Evaluation services, elpi seeks to catalyze a positive transformation in leadership across the continent, ultimately contributing to the realization of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and fostering a culture of excellence and accountability.
