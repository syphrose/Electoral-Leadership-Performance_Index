ECONOMIC GROWTH AWARDS

This pillar is directly aligned with United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 8 (SDG 8), which promotes “sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.” Highlighting this pillar at the Inaugural ELPI Gala is critical, as it underscores the need for leadership that fosters job creation, economic stability, and broad-based prosperity across the continent.

Accelerating Inclusive Economic Growth

Africa's vast coastlines, inland waterways, and marine resources present unparalleled opportunities for economic growth.

Creating Decent Work Opportunities

A major element of SDG 8 is the creation of decent work for all, particularly in regions with high youth unemployment and informal work sectors.

Supporting Innovation and Industrialization

Economic growth must be driven by innovation, technology, and industry to sustain long-term progress.

Reducing Unemployment and Promoting Youth Employment

Youth unemployment is one of the most significant challenges in Africa, with millions of young people entering the workforce each year...

Driving Economic Stability through Leadership

Strong economic growth requires stable governance and sound financial management.
