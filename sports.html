SPORTS AWARDS

The Sports pillar aligns with several United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), including SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being), SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth), and SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities). By promoting sports, leaders contribute to these global objectives, helping to create a more equitable and sustainable future for all. The Gala will highlight the contributions of sports leaders to the SDGs, showcasing how sports can be a powerful vehicle for achieving broader development goals.

Nurturing Leadership and Excellence

Sports are a natural incubator for leadership.

Promoting Social Cohesion and Unity

Sports have the unique ability to unite people across diverse backgrounds, cultures, and communities.

Driving Economic Growth and Development

The sports industry is a significant contributor to economic growth, generating jobs, attracting investment, and driving tourism.

Enhancing Health and Well-being

Sports play a crucial role in promoting health and well-being, combating lifestyle-related diseases, and improving the quality of life.

Empowering Youth and Building Future Leaders

Africa's youth are the continent's greatest asset, and sports offer a powerful platform for their empowerment.
