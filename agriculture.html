AGRICULTURE AWARDS

Food security and agriculture form the backbone of sustainable development in Africa, and ELPI’s focus on this pillar at the Inaugural Gala is crucial in highlighting the importance of resilient agricultural systems and equitable access to food. This aligns with Sustainable Development Goal 2 (SDG 2), which aims to "end hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture."

Addressing Hunger and Malnutrition

Africa struggles with widespread hunger and malnutrition, with millions suffering from food insecurity.

Enhancing Agricultural Productivity

Agriculture is not only a source of food but also a key driver of economic growth in Africa.

Promoting Sustainable Agriculture

Agriculture's environmental impact is significant, and sustainability is key to long-term food security.

Improving Access to Agricultural Inputs and Markets

Smallholder farmers, who form the majority of Africa’s agricultural workforce, often face challenges in accessing markets and necessary inputs like seeds, fertilizers, and technology.

Building Resilience to Climate and Economic Shocks

The frequency of climate-related disasters such as droughts and floods disproportionately affects Africa’s agricultural sector.

Empowering Women and Youth in Agriculture

In Africa, women and youth form the backbone of the agricultural workforce.
